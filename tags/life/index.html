The art of procrastination

 2017-08-21 251 words 2 minutes life procrastination 
I have been thinking of updating my blog for more than a month now and you all guessed it right, I procrastinated. What better way to update it, than write a post about procrastination. When someone asks me “What am I good at”? my immediate answer would be procrastination. I am a full-time procrastinator! I don’t understand why I do it and how I do it, but at the end of the day I realize that I wasted it procrastinating.
